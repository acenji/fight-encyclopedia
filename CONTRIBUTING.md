# Contributing to Fight Encyclopedia

Thank you for your interest in contributing! Fight Encyclopedia is built by **ACENji Tech Solutions Inc.** and we welcome pro bono contributors who want to help document the world's fighting techniques.

## How to Contribute

1. Visit [fightencyclopedia.com/contribute](https://fightencyclopedia.com/contribute)
2. Tell us about your martial arts background
3. Sign our contributor NDA
4. Get assigned to a role that matches your expertise

## Roles We Need

### Martial Arts Researcher (2-3 people)
You train or deeply study a martial art. Help us verify technique names, add missing entries, and ensure accuracy for your style.

**Ideal:** BJJ/MMA/Judo/Karate practitioners, martial arts bloggers, Reddit contributors

### Taxonomy Editor (1 person)
You love organizing knowledge into systems. Review our 7-level technique classification tree and help us get the hierarchy right.

**Ideal:** Librarians, biologists (taxonomy is your language), Wikipedia editors

### Content Writer (2-3 people)
Write clear, engaging descriptions for techniques and martial arts. Turn raw data into encyclopedia-quality articles.

**Ideal:** Martial arts journalists, sports science students, Medium/Substack writers

### Translator (3-5 people)
Help us get original terminology right. Many techniques have Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Russian, Portuguese, or Thai names.

**Ideal:** Native/fluent speakers who also train martial arts. Priority: Japanese, Chinese, Russian, Portuguese, Korean

### Developer (1-2 people)
Help build the platform. Next.js 15, React 19, PostgreSQL, Tailwind.

**Ideal:** Full-stack or frontend devs who want real-world experience on a unique product

### Video Contributor (5+ people)
Find and submit the best instructional videos for each technique.

**Ideal:** Anyone who watches martial arts content on YouTube

### Game Developer (1 person)
Help build Fight IQ — chess-style martial arts puzzles and interactive lessons. The first product of its kind.

**Ideal:** Game developers interested in martial arts + education
